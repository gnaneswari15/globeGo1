Explore Cape Town, South Africa

Trip Details

Discover the beauty of Cape Town on a 6-day tour, where you can explore the stunning landscapes of Table Mountain, Cape Point, and the Winelands. Enjoy the vibrant city life, visit historical sites like Robben Island, and take in the breathtaking views along the coast.

This trip includes guided tours, hotel accommodation, transportation within Cape Town, and entrance to major attractions.

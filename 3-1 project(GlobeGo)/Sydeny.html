Explore Sydney, Australia

Trip Details

Experience a 5-day tour of Sydney, where you can explore the stunning harbor, visit iconic landmarks like the Sydney Opera House and Harbour Bridge, and enjoy the vibrant beaches. The tour includes guided city walks and a day trip to the Blue Mountains.

This trip covers all transportation within the city, hotel accommodation, and entrance fees to top attractions.

Package Options

  • Standard Package: $1500 per person (Includes 3-star hotel, guided tours, and breakfast).
  • Deluxe Package: $2300 per person (Includes 4-star hotel, guided tours, breakfast, and a Sydney Harbor dinner cruise).
  • Premium Package: $4000 per person (Includes 5-star hotel, guided tours, all meals, private yacht tour, and exclusive VIP experiences).
